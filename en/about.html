Who We Are?

Saudi Arabia Leading Business (STAR) is an Egyptian company with Saudi partnership providing international and local business services to companies in several countries, including commercial field and promotion of investment and industrial projects, which helps overcome challenges in various sectors.

Saudi Arabia Leading Business is a comprehensive and leading company in its fields of specialization, providing unique and developed consulting services through modern technologies and methodologies, supported by qualified human resources and the best consultants and experts in various sectors.
